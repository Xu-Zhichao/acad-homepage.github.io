# About Me
- My research interest includes:
- (1) Biochar for soil improvement and Climate change mitigation,
- (2) Soil-biochar-plant machine learning prediction system,
- (3) Trade off between agricultural sustainability and soil health, 
- (4) Sludge pyrolysis resource recovery.
